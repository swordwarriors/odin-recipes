beef pasta

A delicious Beef Pasta Dish

ingredients

  • ground beef
  • your choice of pasta
  • tomato sauce (any tomato based sauce works)
  • your choice of spices/seasonings (i use: cayenne pepper, red chilli, ground cumin, paprika, italian seasoning, and ofcourse salt and pepper)

steps

  1. in a pot of boiling water add your pasta in and salt it (follow the instructions on the pasta box if you are unsure how long to leave it for)
  2. in a hot pan add oil
  3. when the oil is ready add your ground beef (cook on medium to high heat)
  4. move the ground beef for it to cook evenly and add your seasonings to thoroughly mix them
  5. when the beef is done cooking (you will know by its color) add your tomato sauce
  6. let the tomato sauce simmer for a little bit
  7. when you pasta is done cooking drain it and then put it back in the pot
  8. last step is to add the ground beef on top of the pasta and mix it thoroughly, and now you have a delicious pasta dish enjoy :D
