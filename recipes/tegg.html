An egg with tomato recipe

you can never go wrong with a simple recipe and it is delicious, i know it sounds dumb but it is actually delicious and it does sound better in arabic

ingredients

  • eggs
  • tomatoes
  • salt and pepper

steps

  1. in a hot pan add oil
  2. when the oil is ready add your tomatoes first
  3. when the tomatoes start breaking down add your egg
  4. stir the eggs untill they are cooked!
  5. enjoy the food! i recommend eating with arabic style bread
